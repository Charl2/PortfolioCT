Project Overview

Automating the Feedback Loop

This project aimed to enhance the post-mission feedback process through the creation of an NPS V2 form. To streamline communication, I redesigned the workflow for notifying clients at the end of assignments by automating the process with Airtable and Zapier.

The system sent automated emails containing pre-filled forms using URLs that incorporated HubSpot tokens, ensuring data accuracy and pre-populated responses.

NPS Classification

The form was designed to categorise clients based on their feedback as "promoters" (very satisfied), neutral, or dissatisfied. This classification enabled the team to promptly react and engage with clients according to their experience — ensuring tailored follow-ups and improved client relations.

Challenges

Technical Hurdles Solved

Key challenges included integrating HubSpot and Airtable smoothly, managing tokenized URLs, and resolving pre-fill limitations that required extensive research and support. The final solution synchronised Airtable form submissions with HubSpot using Zapier, automating data collection and integration.

The project encompassed developing the NPS form, an automated email template triggered upon mission completion, and ensuring platform synchronisation. Rigorous testing verified correct form pre-filling and data transmission, optimising the feedback process and improving user experience and data management.

The Optimised Workflow

Conclusion

This automation eliminated an entire category of manual work for WEEM's team — dramatically improving feedback collection rates and response consistency. Promoter / Detractor routing meant the team could act on negative feedback within 24 hours of mission completion, improving consultant retention and client relationship quality.
