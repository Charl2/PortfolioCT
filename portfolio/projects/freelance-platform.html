Freelancing Platform
Redesign

A complete UX/UI redesign of WEEM's freelance consultant platform using Figma — aligning with the company's brand identity, restructuring key forms and dashboards, and improving engagement through iterative beta tester feedback.

RoleUX Designer
ToolFigma
CompanyWEEM
My Role

UX Designer, WEEM

I led an extensive project to redesign a freelance platform and its forms using Figma, with a primary focus on enhancing both UX/UI to align with the company's brand identity and key colour scheme. The objective was to craft interactive mockups that demonstrated a user-centric approach and elevated the overall user experience.

A significant part of the redesign involved restructuring the form and dashboard to make them more intuitive and efficient — refining the user flow for easier navigation and reducing friction in key processes, making it simpler for freelancers to manage their tasks and information.

Beta Testing & Iteration

Throughout the project, I incorporated feedback from beta testers, which provided valuable insights into user preferences and pain points. This input informed the addition of new features aimed at improving engagement — enhanced visual cues, clear call-to-action buttons, and streamlined content presentation to boost usability and satisfaction.

Conclusion

This project demonstrated the value of aligning design decisions with both brand identity and user needs. By combining iterative Figma prototyping with structured beta tester feedback, the redesign delivered an experience that was both visually coherent and meaningfully more usable — reducing friction for freelancers managing missions and information day-to-day.
